Goverment engangenment

IYF-Namibia have been enganging with Namibian goverment stakehilders from differents organs and ministries to discuss how to strengthen youth mindset and share common interests within the goverment of the Republic of Namibia

IYF Activies in Namibia

IYF-Namibia encourage strengthen youths mindset by encouring them to participate in physical activies such as Taekwondo, mind games such a Hoola Hoops and many more.

Amplifying Youth Voice

IYF Namibia encourage youth participation in order to share their heart with other youth in Namibia. IYF-Namibia let the voice of the youth to be heard across the Namibian nation in order to shape the future of namibia for the better.

Volunteering

IYF-Namibia is non-profit wordwide NGO and encourages youth to volunteer for the program to facilitate differnt activities of the programs. Volunteers are the intergral part of IYF-Namibia and what are you vaiting for? Join our volunteer team.
